About the Institution

Revista Zenodo is an institutional academic publication founded with the objective of promoting the dissemination of scientific knowledge and establishing meaningful connections between researchers, educational institutions, and civil society.

Our commitment is based on the principles of academic excellence, scientific rigor, and democratization of access to knowledge, seeking to contribute to the advancement of science and the global dissemination of research across a wide range of academic disciplines.
